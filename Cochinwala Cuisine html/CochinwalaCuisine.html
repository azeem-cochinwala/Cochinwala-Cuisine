Background:

Hi my name is Azeem Cochinwala. I am a chef who had a dream to open a luxurious Indian/Pakistan cuisine within the GTA. I wanted to give back to my community by serving the delicous foods of our culture. My dream has finally come true. I have opened a cuisine at the major intersection of Yonge & Dundas, located in the heart of Toronto. Directly facing the eaton center. This restaurant is called Cochinwala Cuisine. My restaurant is open to all, so people from all age groups, income levels and education can come in and enjoy.
